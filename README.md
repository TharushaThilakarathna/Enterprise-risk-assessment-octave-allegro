# Overview

This repository demonstrates the methodology, approach, and professional practices applied during an enterprise cybersecurity risk assessment engagement.

The assessment was conducted using the **OCTAVE Allegro** risk assessment methodology to identify critical information assets, evaluate threat scenarios, analyze business impacts, prioritize risks, and recommend appropriate security controls.

Due to confidentiality obligations and a signed Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), all client-specific information, findings, organizational details, and assessment deliverables have been removed or generalized.

---

# Project Objectives

The primary objectives of the assessment were:

* Identify critical information assets
* Analyze information security risks
* Evaluate potential threat scenarios
* Assess business impact and likelihood
* Prioritize organizational risks
* Recommend appropriate mitigation controls
* Support informed risk management decisions

---

# Methodology

The assessment followed the OCTAVE Allegro framework, which focuses on information assets and organizational risk exposure.

## Assessment Process

```text
Establish Risk Criteria
            ↓
Identify Critical Assets
            ↓
Define Security Requirements
            ↓
Identify Threat Scenarios
            ↓
Analyze Risks
            ↓
Evaluate Impact
            ↓
Prioritize Risks
            ↓
Recommend Controls
```

---

# Activities Performed

## Asset Identification

Identified and profiled critical information assets essential to business operations.

Activities included:

* Asset inventory development
* Asset ownership identification
* Security requirement analysis
* Business dependency assessment

---

## Threat Scenario Development

Developed realistic threat scenarios affecting organizational assets.

Examples of threat sources:

* Insider threats
* External attackers
* Human error
* Process failures
* Technology failures

---

## Business Impact Analysis

Evaluated potential consequences across multiple impact areas.

### Impact Categories

| Impact Area                      | Description                            |
| -------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Reputation & Customer Confidence | Brand reputation and stakeholder trust |
| Financial                        | Revenue loss and operational costs     |
| Productivity                     | Operational disruption and downtime    |
| Legal & Regulatory               | Compliance violations and penalties    |

---

## Risk Analysis

Each threat scenario was evaluated using:

* Probability of occurrence
* Potential business impact
* Risk severity
* Organizational exposure

Risk treatment options included:

* Mitigate
* Accept
* Transfer
* Defer

# Security Domains Covered

The assessment considered security requirements across:

### Confidentiality

Protecting sensitive information from unauthorized disclosure.

### Integrity

Protecting information from unauthorized modification.

### Availability

Ensuring business-critical information remains accessible when required.
