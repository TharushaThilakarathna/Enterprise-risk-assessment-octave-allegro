# Risk Matrix Template

## Overview

A risk matrix is used to evaluate and prioritize risks based on their likelihood of occurrence and potential business impact.

This template can be used during cybersecurity risk assessments, OCTAVE Allegro assessments, threat modeling exercises, and risk management activities.

---

# Risk Matrix

| Impact ↓ / Likelihood → | Low    | Medium | High     |
| ----------------------- | ------ | ------ | -------- |
| High                    | Medium | High   | Critical |
| Medium                  | Low    | Medium | High     |
| Low                     | Low    | Low    | Medium   |

---

# Risk Rating Definitions

## Critical Risk

### Description

Immediate action is required.

### Characteristics

* Severe business impact
* Significant financial loss
* Major operational disruption
* Regulatory consequences
* Reputational damage

### Recommended Action

* Immediate mitigation
* Senior management involvement
* Continuous monitoring

---

## High Risk

### Description

Requires prompt remediation.

### Characteristics

* Noticeable business impact
* Potential service disruption
* Increased security exposure

### Recommended Action

* Prioritize remediation
* Implement security controls
* Regular monitoring

---

## Medium Risk

### Description

Requires management attention.

### Characteristics

* Moderate operational impact
* Manageable business consequences
* Limited exposure

### Recommended Action

* Plan mitigation activities
* Monitor risk levels
* Review periodically

---

## Low Risk

### Description

Minimal impact on business operations.

### Characteristics

* Low probability
* Limited consequences
* Acceptable exposure level

### Recommended Action

* Accept or monitor
* Review during periodic assessments

---

# Impact Assessment Criteria

## High Impact

Examples:

* Major financial loss
* Extended service outage
* Regulatory penalties
* Significant reputational damage
* Loss of sensitive information

---

## Medium Impact

Examples:

* Temporary operational disruption
* Moderate financial impact
* Recoverable service interruption
* Limited customer impact

---

## Low Impact

Examples:

* Minor inconvenience
* Negligible financial impact
* Easily recoverable issues
* Minimal operational effect

---

# Likelihood Assessment Criteria

## High Likelihood

* Expected to occur frequently
* Previously observed multiple times
* Strong threat activity exists

---

## Medium Likelihood

* Could occur under normal conditions
* Known threat capability exists
* Reasonable probability

---

## Low Likelihood

* Unlikely but possible
* Limited threat capability
* Rare occurrence

# Risk Prioritization Workflow

```text
Identify Risk
      ↓
Assess Likelihood
      ↓
Assess Impact
      ↓
Determine Risk Level
      ↓
Prioritize Risk
      ↓
Select Treatment Strategy
      ↓
Monitor & Review
```
