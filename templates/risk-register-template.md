# Risk Register Template

## Overview

A risk register is used to document, assess, prioritize, and track cybersecurity risks identified during a risk assessment process.

# Impact Definitions

## High

* Major financial loss
* Significant operational disruption
* Regulatory penalties
* Severe reputational damage

## Medium

* Noticeable operational impact
* Moderate financial loss
* Limited reputational impact

## Low

* Minimal business disruption
* Negligible financial impact
* Easily recoverable

---

# Likelihood Definitions

## High

Expected to occur frequently or has already occurred multiple times.

## Medium

Could occur under normal operating conditions.

## Low

Unlikely to occur but remains possible.

---

# Risk Treatment Options

## Mitigate

Implement controls to reduce likelihood or impact.

Examples:

* Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
* Security Awareness Training
* Vulnerability Management
* Network Segmentation

---

## Accept

Formally acknowledge and tolerate the risk.

Appropriate when:

* Risk is low
* Treatment cost exceeds benefit

---

## Transfer

Transfer risk to another party.

Examples:

* Cyber Insurance
* Outsourced Services
* Third-Party Security Providers

---

## Avoid

Eliminate the activity causing the risk.

Examples:

* Retire unsupported systems
* Remove unnecessary services

---

# Risk Status Definitions

| Status      | Description                        |
| ----------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Open        | Risk identified but not addressed  |
| In Progress | Mitigation activities underway     |
| Monitoring  | Controls implemented and monitored |
| Closed      | Risk treated and accepted          |
