# Threat Scenario Template

## Overview

A threat scenario describes how a threat actor could exploit a vulnerability to negatively impact an organization's information assets, systems, or business operations.

This template can be used during risk assessments, threat modeling exercises, and OCTAVE Allegro engagements.
